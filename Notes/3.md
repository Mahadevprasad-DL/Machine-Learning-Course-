### **Types of Supervised Learning are**
- **Classification**
- **Regression**

### **What is Classification?**
Classification is about predicting a class or discrete values.  
Example: Male or Female  
![Classification Example](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7e94d3fc-47f4-4574-aefb-48acd1b62cd4)

### **What is Regression?**
Regression is about predicting a quantity or continuous values.  
Example: salary, age  
![Regression Example](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2ef84da2-4d3e-486e-9920-b08b30f60525)

### **Most Important Algorithms for Classification are**
- **Decision Tree Classification**
- **Random Forest Classification**
- **K-Nearest Neighbor**

### **Most Important Algorithms for Regression are**
- **Logistic Regression**
- **Polynomial Regression**
- **Support vector machines**
